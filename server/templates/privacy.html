Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 15, 2026

ResqRide ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and related services for on-demand roadside assistance.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and phone number when you register.
  • Location Data: GPS coordinates to connect you with nearby service providers. Location is collected while the app is in use.
  • Vehicle Information: Make, model, year, and fuel type of vehicles you add to your profile.
  • Vehicle Telematics (Optional): If you choose to connect your vehicle via SmartCar, we access battery level, estimated range, charge status, and odometer data. This data is only collected with your explicit consent.
  • Service History: Records of roadside assistance requests you have made or fulfilled.
  • Payment Information: Transaction amounts and tip data. Full payment card details are handled by our payment processor and are not stored on our servers.
  • Communications: Messages exchanged between drivers and service providers within the app.
  • Device Information: Push notification tokens and device type for delivering alerts.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To match drivers with nearby service providers based on location.
  • To display real-time vehicle data (battery, range, charge status) on your EV dashboard.
  • To process and track roadside assistance requests.
  • To facilitate communication between drivers and providers.
  • To send push notifications about service status, SOS alerts, and job requests.
  • To improve app performance, detect errors, and enhance user experience.
  • To manage premium subscription benefits.

3. Vehicle Data via SmartCar

ResqRide integrates with SmartCar to provide live vehicle telemetry data in the EV Mode feature. When you choose to connect your vehicle:

  • You will be directed to SmartCar's secure authorization flow where you grant explicit permission.
  • We request only the minimum necessary data: battery level, estimated range, charging status, and odometer.
  • Vehicle data is displayed solely within your EV dashboard and is never sold or shared with third parties.
  • You can disconnect your vehicle at any time from the EV Mode screen, which immediately revokes our access.
  • SmartCar's own Privacy Policy applies to their handling of your authorization: smartcar.com/privacy

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal data. We share information only in these circumstances:

  • Between Users: Driver name and location are shared with a service provider when a job is accepted, and vice versa, to facilitate the service.
  • Service Providers: Third-party services (SmartCar, push notification providers, payment processors) used to operate the app.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect the rights and safety of our users.

5. Data Retention

We retain your account information for as long as your account is active. Service history and chat messages are stored for up to 12 months. Vehicle telemetry data is not permanently stored — it is fetched on demand and displayed in real time. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us.

6. Security

We use industry-standard security measures including encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS), secure token storage, and access controls. OAuth tokens for vehicle access are stored securely on our servers and are never exposed to third parties.

7. Children's Privacy

ResqRide is not intended for users under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Revoke vehicle access permissions at any time through the app.
  • Opt out of push notifications through your device settings.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or via email. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
