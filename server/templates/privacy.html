3

Location Data

Location is the most sensitive data we handle. Here is the exact timeline of when it is active:

When you request a service Your GPS coordinates are captured once to find providers near you. You can also type an address manually.
While a service is active Your location is shared with your assigned provider so they can navigate to you. Their location is shared back so you can track their ETA.
During SOS / Emergency Mode If you activate Emergency Mode, your coordinates are sent to the emergency contacts you have saved. This is the only time your location reaches a third party (your own contacts).
At all other times Location is not accessed. No background tracking. No location history. No sharing with advertisers.

  • Provide, operate, and improve the Service

Payments & Stripe

All payment card processing is handled by Stripe, Inc. (PCI-DSS Level 1). When you add a payment method, your card number goes directly into Stripe's secure form — it never passes through ResqRide's servers. We store only your Stripe customer ID and last four digits for display.

For providers receiving payouts, Stripe Connect handles identity verification and bank account storage. ResqRide stores only your Stripe account ID.

7

Data Retention

  • Account data: Retained until you delete your account, then erased within 30 days
  • Service records: Retained for 3 years for billing disputes, then deleted
  • Support chat: Retained for 12 months, then deleted
  • Session tokens: Expire after 30 days of inactivity
  • Location: Service address stored with the service record; raw GPS not retained beyond the active session
8

Security

  • Passwords hashed with scrypt — even ResqRide cannot read your password
  • All data transmitted over HTTPS / TLS
  • Session tokens stored in device secure storage (Keychain on iOS, Keystore on Android)
  • Database access restricted to server-side processes only
  • Rate limiting on authentication endpoints to prevent brute-force attacks

If you discover a security vulnerability, please report it to privacy@resqride.co.
