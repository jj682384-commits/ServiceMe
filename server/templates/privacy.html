2. Location Data — Detailed Explanation

Location is the most sensitive data we handle. We want to be completely transparent about how it works.

When location is collected:

  • Driver — Requesting service: Your location is collected when you open a service request so we can find providers near you. It is shared with your assigned provider only while the job is active.
  • Provider — On duty: Your location is periodically updated so drivers can see your ETA and approach. It is only visible to a driver who has an active job with you.
  • SOS Emergency Mode: If you activate the SOS feature, your location is shared continuously until you end the emergency. This is the only scenario where background location is used, and only with your explicit prior consent.

When location is NOT collected:

  • When the app is closed or in the background (except during an active SOS).
  • When you are signed in but not actively using a service or on duty.
  • We never track you passively or build a historical location profile.

Who sees your location:

  • Only the matched driver or provider for an active job — no one else.
  • Location data is never sold, shared with advertisers, or used for profiling.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Match drivers with nearby, qualified service providers.
  • Display real-time provider location and ETA during a job.
  • Process and track roadside assistance requests end-to-end.
  • Facilitate secure in-app chat between drivers and providers.
  • Send push notifications for service status, SOS alerts, and job requests.
  • Manage premium membership benefits and billing.
  • Verify provider identity and professional credentials.
  • Improve app performance and fix errors using anonymized usage data.

We do not use your data for advertising, sell it to data brokers, or share it with any third party for their own commercial purposes.

4. Sharing of Information

We share the minimum necessary data only in these circumstances:

  • Between matched users: Driver name, vehicle info, and approximate location are shared with an accepted provider. Provider name, vehicle, rating, and ETA are shared with the driver. This ends when the job completes.
  • Payment processing: Stripe handles all payment card data under their own strict PCI-DSS compliance. We share only transaction amounts.
  • Push notifications: Apple Push Notification Service (APNs) delivers alerts using your device token. Apple does not receive the content of the notification.
  • AI support assistant: Support chat messages are sent to OpenAI to generate helpful responses. They are not used to train OpenAI models.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or to protect the safety of our users.

5. Payment Data & Security

ResqRide uses Stripe for all payment processing. When you add a payment method:

  • Your card number, CVV, and expiry are entered directly into Stripe's secure interface — we never see them.
  • We store only your Stripe Customer ID (a reference token) in our database.
  • Providers who choose to receive payouts connect a Stripe Express account — their bank details are held by Stripe, not us.
  • All transactions use HTTPS/TLS encryption in transit.

6. Vehicle Telematics (SmartCar)

ResqRide optionally integrates with SmartCar to provide live vehicle data in the EV Mode feature.

  • This is entirely opt-in — the app works fully without it.
  • You authorize access through SmartCar's own secure OAuth flow.
  • We request only: battery level, estimated range, charge status, and odometer.
  • This data is shown only on your EV dashboard and never sold or shared.
  • You can disconnect your vehicle at any time from the EV Mode screen, which immediately revokes our access.

SmartCar's Privacy Policy: smartcar.com/privacy

7. Data Retention

  • Account data is retained for as long as your account is active.
  • Service history and receipts are retained for 24 months, then anonymized.
  • In-app chat messages are retained for 12 months.
  • Provider location data is not stored — it is only held in memory while active and not written to our database.
  • Vehicle telematics are fetched on demand in real time and never stored on our servers.
  • You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.

8. Security Measures

  • All data in transit is protected with HTTPS/TLS encryption.
  • Passwords are hashed with scrypt — they are never stored in plain text and are not recoverable even by our team.
  • Session tokens are stored securely using device-level secure storage (iOS Keychain / Android Keystore).
  • Our PostgreSQL database is hosted in a private network with restricted access.
  • We conduct regular security reviews and patch vulnerabilities promptly.

9. Children's Privacy

ResqRide is not intended for users under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately and we will delete it.

10. Your Rights & Choices

You have full control over your data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Update your name, phone, or email in the app at any time.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and all associated data by emailing us.
  • Notification opt-out: Turn off push notifications in your device settings or inside the app.
  • Location opt-out: Revoke location access in your device settings at any time (note: this limits core functionality).
  • Vehicle disconnect: Disconnect SmartCar access from the EV Mode screen.
  • Contacts: We never access your contacts without a direct action from you.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top and notify you through the app for significant changes. Continued use of ResqRide after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
