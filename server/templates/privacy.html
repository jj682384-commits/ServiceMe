Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 15, 2026  ·  Effective: June 15, 2026

🛡️

The short version: ResqRide only collects what it needs to connect you with roadside help. We never sell your data. Location is only active during a service request. Payments go directly to Stripe — we never see your card number. You can delete your account and all data at any time.

1 Introduction

ResqRide ("we," "our," or "us") operates the ResqRide mobile application and website (collectively, the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, who we share it with, and what choices you have.

By creating an account or using the Service, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the Service.

3 Location Data

Location is the most sensitive data we handle. Here is the exact timeline of when it is active:

📍
When you request a service Your GPS coordinates are captured once to find providers near you and show them on the map. You can also type an address manually instead.
🗺️
While a service is active Your location is shared with your assigned provider so they can navigate to you. The provider's location is shared back with you so you can see their ETA on the map.
🆘
During SOS / Emergency Mode If you activate Emergency Mode, your coordinates and a Google Maps link are sent to the emergency contacts you have saved. This is the only time your location is sent to a third party (your own contacts).
🚫
At all other times Location is not accessed. We do not track you in the background, we do not build location history, and we do not share your location with advertisers — ever.

Location data is stored as part of the service record (the address of where you requested help) but raw GPS coordinates are not retained beyond the active session.

6 Payments & Stripe

All payment card processing is handled by Stripe, Inc., a PCI-DSS Level 1 certified payment processor. When you add a payment method:

  • Your card number is entered directly into Stripe's secure form — it never passes through ResqRide's servers
  • ResqRide stores only your Stripe customer ID and the last four digits of your card for display purposes
  • Your full card number, CVV, and expiry are never visible to ResqRide employees or stored in our database

For providers receiving payouts, Stripe Connect is used. Stripe handles identity verification and bank account storage in compliance with financial regulations. ResqRide stores only your Stripe account ID.

7 Data Retention

We keep your data for as long as your account is active. Specific retention periods:

  • Account data: Retained until you delete your account, then permanently erased within 30 days
  • Service records: Retained for 3 years for billing dispute resolution, then deleted
  • Support chat: Retained for 12 months, then deleted
  • Session tokens: Automatically expire after 30 days of inactivity
  • Location data: Address of service stored with service record; raw GPS coordinates not retained beyond the active session

8 Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Passwords hashed with scrypt — even ResqRide cannot read your password
  • All data transmitted over HTTPS/TLS
  • Bearer tokens stored in device secure storage (Keychain on iOS, Keystore on Android)
  • Database access restricted to server-side processes only
  • Rate limiting on authentication endpoints to prevent brute-force attacks

No system is 100% secure. If you discover a vulnerability, please report it to privacy@resqride.co.
