How It Works

Help in 3 simple steps

From breakdown to back on the road — fast, transparent, and stress-free.

01
📱

Pick, Price & Pay

Choose the service you need — flat tire (spare swap or inflation), jump start, fuel, lockout, tow, OBD scan, or EV charging. Select your sub-options, confirm your GPS location, and see your exact price upfront. Add your payment method and submit. You're charged before anyone is dispatched — no surprises.

02
📍

Track Them Live

A verified nearby provider accepts your job and heads your way. Watch their exact location move toward you on the map in real time, see their live ETA, and message them directly in-app if you need to.

03
✅

Fixed. Tip. Done.

Your provider arrives, completes the work, and marks the job done. Add a tip if you'd like, get your digital receipt instantly, and rate the experience — then you're back on the road.
