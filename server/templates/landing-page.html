How It Works

Help in 3 simple steps

From breakdown to back on the road — fast, transparent, and stress-free.

01
📱

Pick Your Service

Tap the service you need — flat tire, jump start, fuel, lockout, OBD scan, or EV charging. Set your options (fuel amount, charge level, add-ons), confirm your GPS location, and see your exact price before you request.

02
📍

Track Them Live

A verified nearby provider accepts your job and heads your way. Watch their exact location move toward you on the map, see their live ETA, and message them directly in-app if you need to.

03
✅

Fixed. Pay. Go.

Your provider completes the job and marks it done. Add a tip, pay securely in-app, and get a digital receipt in seconds. Rate the experience — then you're back on the road.
