DATA SAFETY

Your Data, Protected

Plain language. No fine print. Here is exactly what ResqRide does and does not do with your information.

Last updated: June 14, 2026
🔒

We Never Sell Your Data

Your personal information is never sold to advertisers, data brokers, or any third party. Ever.

📍

Location Only When Needed

We only access your location during active use — to find providers near you or share your ETA. Never in the background unless you activate SOS.

💳

We Never See Your Card

Payment card numbers go directly to Stripe. We never see, store, or handle your full card details.

🗑️

Delete Anytime

Email us and we will permanently delete your account and all associated data within 30 days.

🔐

Passwords Are Unreadable

Your password is hashed with scrypt. Even our own team cannot see it. If you forget it, we can only reset it — not recover it.

👤

Minimum Data Only

We only request the permissions the app actually needs. We do not access your camera, contacts, or photos without a direct action from you.

Location — The Full Picture

Location is the permission people ask about most. Here is exactly when we do and do not access it:

Requesting a serviceYour GPS is used to find providers near you. Shared with your matched provider once they accept.
Provider on dutyProvider location is updated periodically so drivers can see ETA. Only visible to a driver with an active job.
SOS Emergency Mode (background)Background location is used only when you manually activate SOS. It stops the moment you end the emergency.
All other times — nothingWhen the app is closed, in the background (outside SOS), or when you are logged in but not in a session, we do not access your location.

Bottom line: We never build a location history. We never track you for advertising. Location is used only to connect you with help and show your provider how to reach you.

What We Do NOT Collect

  • Browsing history or activity outside ResqRide
  • Your full contact list (only a single contact you choose to import)
  • Microphone audio (permission is declared for potential future voice messages but not currently used)
  • Precise location when the app is not in active use (except SOS)
  • Any data from children under 16
  • Advertising identifiers or tracking pixels

Third-Party Services We Use

  • Stripe — Payment processing. PCI-DSS Level 1 certified. stripe.com/privacy
  • Apple Push Notification Service (APNs) — Delivers push notifications. Apple does not receive the content of your notifications.
  • OpenAI (gpt-4o) — Powers the in-app support chat assistant. Support messages are processed but not used to train models. openai.com/policies/privacy-policy
  • SmartCar — Optional vehicle telematics. Only connected with your explicit authorization. smartcar.com/privacy
  • RevenueCat — Manages in-app subscription entitlements. revenuecat.com/privacy

None of these partners receive your data for their own advertising or profiling purposes.

Controls You Have Right Now

  • Turn off location — iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Location Services → ResqRide → Never (or "While Using")
  • Turn off notifications — iOS Settings → Notifications → ResqRide → toggle off, or inside the app under Settings
  • Disconnect vehicle telematics — ResqRide → EV Mode → Disconnect SmartCar
  • Remove payment method — ResqRide → Settings → Payment Methods → Remove
  • Delete your account — Email privacy@resqride.co and we will permanently delete everything within 30 days

We take data privacy seriously. Contact our privacy team and we'll respond within 2 business days.
